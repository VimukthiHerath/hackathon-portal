how submission works

From solve to scoreboard, no manual grading

  1. Download & solve

    Grab the starter pack for an unlocked question on the Questions page and solve it locally.

  2. Zip your solution

    Compress your solution file into a standard .zip — nothing else needed.

  3. Open a submission issue

    Pick your question from the dropdown and attach your zip. No account setup, no forms outside GitHub.

  4. Get your result

    A comment lands on your issue with a pass/fail and your score, usually within a couple of minutes.
